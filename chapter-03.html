Guard your time and your dynamics

TimeFocusRoutineFramework

I often, in fact almost every day struggle with proper time management, in the sense that I want to spend my time in the most useful way possible. However this is extremely difficult to perform and oftentimes leads to frustration. I wonder, what is the right move here?

As mentioned there is only so much I can influence, right? I have my job... and on top of it? More often than not jobs are repetitive, and require us to assimilate a well-performing set of behavior to maximize efficiency.. now what if you approximated such a behavior to your own good? Clearly this is easier said, finding your genie is all it takes.

The first step is to see what is ahead, look and really see things you also don't want to see. Second, you have to be in a state of openness, receptiveness - just like when foraging for mushrooms - to spot the right ones you are looking for. Your eyes will clearly see other mushrooms but you will ignore them as you are focused on finding desired one. Ultimately, this is really about not just finding and doing what you want, but also becoming aware of alternatives, distractions and things of less importance. Another example could be like looking at a painting and mostly it is never about what you see on the canvas, but time, ideas & emotions, context, values and materials. Giving your actions this thought process does make a difference. Consider this.
